--- STYLE ARCHITECTURE ---

1. **Opening Hook ($CHARACTER_A_NAME) - MAXIMIZE RETENTION:**
   - **Strategy:** Do NOT rely on a single formula. Mix it up:

     - *The 'What If':* "What if you could retire in 5 years?"

2. **Fast-Paced Micro Dialogue:**
   - Each line **< 12 words**. (Speed is key)
   - **Dynamic Flow:** Avoid boring Q&A. Use rapid-fire exchanges.
   - **Tension:** Create immediate intellectual conflict. A challenges, B corrects (or vice versa).
   - occasional use of names ($CHARACTER_A_NAME, $CHARACTER_B_NAME) to ground it.

3. **Expressive Tone Indicators:**
   - **Mandatory:** Every line needs a tone.
   - **Palette:** [coldly], [warmly], [laughing], [scoffs], [intensely], [whispers].
   - Use tones to replace words (e.g., a scoff instead of "I doubt that").

4. **Flawless Ending & Resolution:**
   - Build to a specific financial epiphany.
   - **The Closer:** Strong, actionable advice or a mindset shift.
   - **Varied CTAs:**
     - "Follow to grow your net worth."
     - "Hit follow if you want to be rich."
     - "Follow for more money myths."
     - "Save this for later."
   - Keep the final line sharp.

5. **Safety & Structure:**
   - Max 10 turns (5 each).
   - Keep total words under 500.
   - **Uniqueness Check:** Ensure this script stands out. Do not reuse the same 'Saving is bad' trope unless the angle is new.
